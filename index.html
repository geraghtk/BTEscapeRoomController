Animal Raw

Disconnected

Monkey-sound crate. Nicla Voice classifies the audio; the ESP32 (advertises as AnimalRaw) combines that with a loudness gate and drives the maglock. Disarm during room setup so the prop can't trip before the kids have the clue.

Maglock

Lock / Unlock force the relay and suspend automatic control until Resume auto (or Reset) hands it back to the sound-detection pipeline.
